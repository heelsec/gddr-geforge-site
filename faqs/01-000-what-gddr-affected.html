What is GDDR6? Am I affected? Just like how computers use some amount of DRAM for their physical memory, GPUs also use DRAM for their own physical memory. The form factor is different, however, thus the prefix GDDR. GDDR6 is a specification for this memory type, and is the main type of GDRR in GPUs released in the last few years. We believe any system employing modern GPU with GDDR6 memory may be susceptible to this attack.
